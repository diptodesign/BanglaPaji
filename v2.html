    Gezer Calendar (Canaanite)

    The Gezer Calendar is one of the oldest known Hebrew inscriptions (10th century BCE), describing agricultural seasons:

    1. Yarcho Asif - Two months: Ingathering (Tishrei-Cheshvan)
    2. Yarcho Zera - Two months: Sowing (Kislev-Tevet)
    3. Yarcho Lakish - Two months: Late Sowing (Shevat-Adar)
    4. Yerach Ketzir Seorim - One month: Barley Harvest (Nisan)
    5. Yerach Ketzir Vechag - One month: Harvest and Festival (Iyar)
    6. Yarcho Zamir - Two months: Vine Pruning (Sivan-Tammuz)
    7. Yerach Kayitz - One month: Summer Fruit (Av-Elul)

    Umma Calendar (Sumerian)

    The Umma calendar from ancient Sumer (c. 2100 BCE) had 12 lunar months:

    1. Bara-za-gar
    2. Ezen-gu-si-su
    3. Ezen-Šu-numun
    4. Ezen-Šu-numun-min
    5. Ezen-Ne-Ne-gar
    6. Ezen-Kin-Inanna
    7. Ezen-Du-ku
    8. Ezen-Apin-du-a
    9. Ezen-Gan-gan-na
    10. Ezen-Ab-e
    11. Ezen-Udru-du-a
    12. Ezen-Me-ki-gal
